Series Template

The page for a multi-episode teaching arc. Bound to /ministry/[slug]/series/[seriesSlug]. The user's path through a series is tracked — episodes show watch state, the next unwatched episode is highlighted, and completed series are marked.

One sentence

Series pages are ordered, sequential, stateful. Unlike most templates where the Library reorders content per user, the episode list is fixed by sequence. Personalization shows up only in "Continue watching" prominence and related-series recommendations.

Annotated walkthrough

1
Breadcrumbs · 3 levels Series live under ministries. The breadcrumb walks Home → Ministries → Ministry name → Series name. Each level navigates back to its respective page.
2
Hero-Series · with primary CTA Series art, name, ministry attribution, description, and stats. Importantly, the primary CTA reads "Continue from episode 3" for returning users — the renderer reads content_state to know where the user left off and surfaces the resume button. Cold-start visitors see "Start episode 1".
3
Progress tracker · personalized Aggregated from content_state rows for each episode in the series. Shows total completed / total episodes plus current in-progress detail. Hidden for cold-start visitors and replaced with: "Series overview".
4
EpisodeList · ordered, stateful, not reordered This is the only template where the Library does not reorder content. Series have a sequence; the sequence is sacred. Each row shows: number, thumbnail (with play/completed icon), title, description, watch state, duration, and a thin progress bar if in-progress. The current episode is highlighted with a gold border.
5
Ministry context · always present A compact MinistryCard reminds the user whose teaching this is and offers a path back to the ministry page (M3.5). Helps users discover more from a pastor they're connecting with.
6
Related series · personalized DYNAMIC slot The only personalized part of the page. Library decides what fits — related series (SeriesRail) for users who like long-form, or single related teachings (ContentRail) for users who prefer one-off content. The framing copy on the second rail ("If forgiveness is heavy right now") is Library-authored based on the user's recent emotional-pain signals.

The "no reordering" rule

Series episodes are sequential

Unlike every other content slot in the system, EpisodeList does not re-rank by user signals. Episode 4 must always appear after Episode 3. The Library will not return episodes in a different order based on personalization. However, the resume CTA in the hero, the progress tracker, and which episode is highlighted ARE personalized from content_state.

State management

Per-user state for the series

StateSourceEffect on render
Not startedNo content_state rows for any episodeHero CTA: "Start episode 1" · No progress tracker
In progressAt least one episode with watch_progress > 0 and < 1Hero CTA: "Continue from episode N" · Progress tracker shown · Current episode highlighted
CompleteAll episodes with watch_progress ≥ 0.95Hero CTA: "Watch again from episode 1" · "✓ Series complete" badge · Related series promoted
SavedSeries saved (separate from content_state)Save button toggled · Series appears in My Journey saved list

Anonymous user behavior

Free browsing requires no signup (YouTube TOS · CLAUDE.md). Anonymous users get a degraded but functional Series page:

  • Progress tracker hidden (no identity = no per-user state to render)
  • Hero CTA reads "Start episode 1" regardless of session
  • No completion / in-progress badges
  • Related series still personalized based on anonymousId behavioral signals (cookies)

When the user signs up, their pre-signup watch history (tagged with anonymousId) merges into their userId via the identity merge protocol (M1.1 §12). On their next visit, the Series page reflects their accumulated state.

Auto-advance behavior

When a user finishes an episode, the embedded YouTube player triggers a video_completed event. The renderer (client-side) checks the EpisodeList config:

  • If autoplayNext = true AND there is a next episode, a countdown overlay appears: "Next: Episode 4 — Forgiveness Without Reconciliation · starting in 5s"
  • User can cancel ("Watch later") or accept (clicks play)
  • On accept, navigates to the next episode's video player route
  • If it's the final episode, the overlay shows: "Series complete. See related series" + scrolls related-series rail into view

Empty & edge states

Series has 1 episode

Hero CTA reads "Watch" (not "Start episode 1"). EpisodeList still renders but feels heavy — admin guidance: single-episode series should be tagged as standalone content instead.

Series not yet complete (ongoing)

Stats line shows "5 of 12 episodes · ongoing". The final placeholder episode rows show "Coming soon" with no thumbnail.

Episode is tier-gated

Episode renders with a lock icon on the thumbnail and an UpgradeTeaser inline below the row. Click → upgrade flow, not video player.

Series unavailable (ministry permission pulled)

404 with a fallback page suggesting similar series.
